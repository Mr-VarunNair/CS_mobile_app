What is there in the Mobile App? For students

The Cyber Square Mobile App brings learning and interactive experiences right to your fingertips! With an intuitive interface and seamless navigation, the app offers a range of features to enhance learning and engagement.

Get Started

Install the Cyber Square Mobile App

Enhance your learning experience with the Cyber Square mobile app! Follow the steps below to get started:


Android Users


  1. Scan the QR Code: Use your smartphone’s camera or QR code scanner to scan the Android QR code below.

  2. Download and Install: Tap the link that appears to open the Google Play Store. Click Install and wait for the app to download.

  3. Open the App: Once installed, launch the Cyber Square app and explore its features.

iOS Users


  1. Scan the QR Code: Use your smartphone’s camera or QR code scanner to scan the iOS QR code below.

  2. Download and Install: Tap the link that appears to open the App Store. Click Install and wait for the app to download.

  3. Open the App: Once installed, launch the Cyber Square app and explore its features.
